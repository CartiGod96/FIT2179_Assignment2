The Race to the Finals

This interactive bump chart tracks the round-by-round trajectory of the ladder rankings. Hover over any line to isolate a team's path.

The Home Court Fortress

Playing in the NBL requires brutal transcontinental travel. Here, we project stadium footprints across Australia, where circle sizes represent stadium capacities.

Player Offensive Profiles

Who are the true offensive anchors of the season? This visualization maps player workloads against scoring output. Use the dropdown to filter by franchise.

True Shooting Efficiency

Traditional field goal metrics fail to account for the added value of the three-point shot. This chart isolates Effective Field Goal Percentage (eFG%), adjusting for the fact that a made shot from deep is worth 50% more than a standard field goal.

The Possession Frontier

Points Per Possession (PPP) pits offensive execution against defensive resilience. Franchises sitting toward the top-left generate more output than they surrender, identifying true title contenders.

The Geometry of Shot Selection

Where do teams hunt their points? This spatial scatter plot renders thousands of individual real-world shot locations mapped directly over the court hardwood. Use the dropdown selector to isolate franchises.
