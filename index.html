The Race to the Finals

A basketball season isn't won in a single round. Aggregated end-of-year standings often mask the chaotic shifts in momentum, injury crises, and tactical adjustments that teams undergo across a campaign.

This interactive bump chart tracks the round-by-round trajectory of the ladder rankings. Hover over any team's line to isolate their path and filter out league noise to witness their competitive velocity.
