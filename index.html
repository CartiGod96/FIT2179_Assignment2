A Spatial and Statistical Study of the 2025-2026 Season

Data Visualisation Portfolio by Liam Fowler

The Race to the Finals

This interactive bump chart tracks the round-by-round trajectory of the ladder rankings. Hover over any team's line to isolate their path and witness their competitive velocity.

The Home Court Fortress

Playing in the NBL requires brutal transcontinental travel. Here, we project stadium footprints across Australia.

The circle sizes represent stadium capacities, contrasting massive metropolitan hubs against smaller regional environments.

Player Offensive Profiles

Who are the true offensive anchors of the season? This visualization maps player workloads against scoring output.

Use the dropdown menu below the chart to filter the league by team to isolate specific roster performance metrics.

True Shooting Efficiency

Traditional field goal metrics fail to account for the added value of the three-point shot. This chart isolates Effective Field Goal Percentage (eFG%), adjusting for the fact that a made shot from deep is worth 50% more than a standard field goal.

Teams are sorted dynamically from highest to lowest efficiency. The dashed red line represents the calculated league benchmark.

The Possession Frontier

Points Per Possession (PPP) is the definitive measure of efficiency in modern basketball. This matrix pits offensive execution against defensive resilience.

The diagonal baseline marks net-zero efficiency. Franchises sitting above the line generate more output than they surrender per possession, identifying true title contenders. Hover over a dot to highlight a specific franchise.

The Geometry of Shot Selection

Where do teams hunt their points? This spatial scatter plot renders thousands of individual real-world shot locations mapped directly over the court hardwood.

Use the dropdown selector to compare how team shot distributions and shot-making heat spots change between different franchises.
